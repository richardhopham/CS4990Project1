       <h2 class="featurette-heading">Our Campus, Your Future <span class="text-muted"></span> </h2> 
       <p class="lead"> </p>
       <p><strong>Feb. 6 from 10:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. / BSC, Ursa Major</strong></p> 
       <p>If you’re interested in developing leadership skills, improving your health and learning about new digital tools, be sure to <span><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/our-campus-your-future/registration.shtml">register</a></span> for the Our Campus, Your Future summit. (Drop-ins are welcome, but registration ensures a complimentary lunch.) The all-star cast includes <span><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/our-campus-your-future/matt-sakaguchi.shtml">Matt Sakaguchi</a></span> from Google, <span><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/our-campus-your-future/ryan-zervakos.shtml">Ryan Zervakos</a></span> from LinkedIn, and <span><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/our-campus-your-future/keynote.shtml">keynote speaker Shola Richards</a></span>. We hope to see you there, but if you’re unable to attend, follow <span><a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.instagram.com%2Fcppeoda%2F&amp;data=02%7C01%7Cmmquiroz%40cpp.edu%7C1d6aca2622da4a0840dd08d6856f22d5%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636843112470781884&amp;sdata=SchCU6MQB8H0kRjVHvJNwVIGuhpfVeBS5utTWZlP05g%3D&amp;reserved=0">Employee &amp; Organizational Development &amp; Advancement (@cppeoda) on Instagram</a></span> for updates.</p>
