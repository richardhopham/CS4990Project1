       <p><strong><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/~cppmag/career-by-design.shtml" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><img alt="Barbara Bouza" height="268" src="https://www.cpp.edu/alumni/img/spotlight/bouza1.jpg" width="370"></a></strong></p> 
       <p>Not many teens navigating the hallowed halls of high school know definitively what they want to do for a career.<br><br>Growing up in Santa Maria, Barbara Bouza (’85, architecture) loved art, creative problem solving, and math, and she also had an analytical side.<br><br>It wasn’t until an architecture class in high school that Bouza realized she could pursue a profession that would tap into all of her passions.<br><br>“I took the course and I knew I wanted to be an architect,” she says.</p>
