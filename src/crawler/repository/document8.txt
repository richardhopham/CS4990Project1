       <h2 class="featurette-heading">Cal Poly Pomona Tasting &amp; Auction <span class="text-muted"></span> </h2> 
       <p class="lead"> The annual Cal Poly Pomona Tasting &amp; Auction is an unforgettable, open-air gathering in the picturesque Kellogg Pomona Rose Garden and Aratani Japanese Garden. Guests mingle with friends, savor fabulous wines, sample wonderful craft beer and taste enticing cuisine from some of Southern California’s most popular restaurants, while enjoying lively musical and auction entertainment. Proceeds will benefit Cal Poly Pomona and its 24,000 students with academic enrichment opportunities.</p>

      <div class="col-md-7 order-2"> 
       <h2 class="featurette-heading">Career Center <span class="text-muted"></span> </h2> 
       <p class="lead"> The Career Center’s goal is to provide students with resources and assistance to address all aspects of planning their career and searching for a job. Students can navigate a variety of part-time and full-time jobs or internships via Handshake, the Career Center’s job posting board. The Clothes Closet provides students with professional attire needed for interviews and career-related activities. Drop-in peer advisors are ready to review resumes and cover letters while career counselors work with individuals to explore the best major based upon interests and career goals. Take advantage of career fairs during the semester to help launch your career.</p>
