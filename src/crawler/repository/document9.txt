         <header class="entry-header"> 
          <h1 class="entry-title">Spring Pizza with the Presidents on March 21</h1> 
          <div class="entry-byline">
           Posted on 
           <time class="published" datetime="2019-03-07T08:15:13-07:00" title="Thursday, March 7th, 2019, 8:15 am">March 7, 2019</time>
          </div> 
          <!--ADDED BY LINDSEY-->
          <hr> 
          <div class="post-thumbnail-wrap">
           <img width="640" height="360" src="http://polycentric.cpp.edu/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/18pizza-Prez-Coley3.jpg" class="attachment-post-thumbnail featured-image wp-post-image" alt="President Soraya Coley answers questions during Pizza with the Presidents.">
          </div>
          <!-- .post-thumbnail-wrap -->
          <figure class="wp-caption">
           President Soraya Coley answers questions during Pizza with the Presidents.
          </figure> 
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header --> 
         <div class="entry-content"> 
          <p>University President Soraya M. Coley and ASI President Jenny Greenberg invite students to enjoy free pizza as they ask questions of them and other university and student leaders.</p> 
          <p>The spring semester Pizza with the Presidents will take place on <strong>Thursday, March 21</strong>, from 12 to 1 p.m. (U-Hour) in the University Quad. The university will provide free pizza, lemonade, iced tea and water to students.</p> 
          <p><strong>How to Ask a Question</strong></p> 
          <p>Question/comment cards will be available at the event, or questions may be submitted in advance to the <u><a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.twitter.com%2Fcalpolypomona&amp;data=02%7C01%7C%7C8c8b10501aab4f3dc0a508d624b6dba7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636736765108605777&amp;sdata=AzC0rmWNnhZUJXTVEji%2BLUYLOgFXJ5oUvVWTZ28EszM%3D&amp;reserved=0">@calpolypomona</a></u> Twitter account with the hashtag <u><a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftwitter.com%2Fhashtag%2FAskColey%3Fsrc%3Dhash&amp;data=02%7C01%7C%7C8c8b10501aab4f3dc0a508d624b6dba7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636736765108605777&amp;sdata=Mh2OPxWWXIMjq6v3l1BWGgqCXVxkbElL0na0Z%2B9GxTw%3D&amp;reserved=0">#askColey</a></u> or <u><a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Ftwitter.com%2Fhashtag%2FASKASI%3Fsrc%3Dhash&amp;data=02%7C01%7C%7C8c8b10501aab4f3dc0a508d624b6dba7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636736765108605777&amp;sdata=kqWH%2FM7P%2F1t7eC0fegQLWZV9xKUrqeJhst5Ao68wSdc%3D&amp;reserved=0">#askASI</a></u>.</p> 
          <p>During the Q&amp;A, student leaders will receive the questions and provide them to the moderator, interim Dean of Students Benardo Dargan.</p> 
          <p>Because the event is limited to about an hour, there might not be enough time to address all questions. On the comment cards, students may write their CPP email address if they wish to receive a response after the event.</p> 
          <p>A recap of the event, as well as answers to all questions, will be posted on <u><a href="http://polycentric.cpp.edu/">PolyCentric</a></u> later in the weeks following.</p> 
          <p><strong>Follow the Q&amp;A on Social Media</strong></p> 
          <p>Pizza with the Presidents will be streamed live through Cal Poly Pomonas <u><a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fcalpolypomona&amp;data=02%7C01%7C%7C8c8b10501aab4f3dc0a508d624b6dba7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636736765108605777&amp;sdata=XdIToG5bstxc39m33Gob7VJ4FDgVKNMWjq5cSXHHXgI%3D&amp;reserved=0">Facebook</a></u> and <u><a href="http://polycentric.cpp.edu/2018/09/fall-pizza-with-the-presidents-set-for-oct-2/video.cpp.edu/live">YouTube</a></u> accounts. On <u><a href="https://na01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.twitter.com%2Fcalpolypomona&amp;data=02%7C01%7C%7C8c8b10501aab4f3dc0a508d624b6dba7%7C164ba61e39ec4f5d89ffaa1f00a521b4%7C0%7C0%7C636736765108605777&amp;sdata=AzC0rmWNnhZUJXTVEji%2BLUYLOgFXJ5oUvVWTZ28EszM%3D&amp;reserved=0">Twitter</a></u>, the questions and answers will be posted in real time.</p> 
          <p>Later this semester, an evening Pizza with the Presidents event will be held on April 10, from 6 to 7 p.m.</p> 
          <p>The event is hosted by the Office of the President and ASI.</p> 
         </div>
